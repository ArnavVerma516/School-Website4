Jivitesh Singh-The boy who speaks through his Camera

Jivitesh Singh-The boy who speaks through his Camera

Jivitesh Singh, a student of Class-VII started taking photographs of wildlife, nature and birds when he was just four years old. Recently, his interview as the Youngest Wildlife Photographer of India was telecast on DD National Channel. He got selected and was appreciated in 3rd National Art Exhibition, 2017 at All India Fine Arts and Crafts Society, wherein he Won Gold Medal and received a Letter of Appreciation from Director General, NIA, Ministry of Home Affairs, Government of India in the Group Art Exhibition 2018. He was awarded as the Youngest Wildlife Photographer of India by India Book of Records, Global Records and Research Foundation. His name is enlisted in Assist Book of World Record as the ‘Youngest Birder’. He was also awarded with ‘Pearl of Nation Award’ in the field of wildlife photography.
